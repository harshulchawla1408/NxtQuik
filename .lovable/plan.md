

# NXTQUIK – IT Services & Creative Content Website

## Overview
A modern, professional single-page website for NXTQUIK with a blue gradient color palette derived from the logo, clean white backgrounds, glassmorphism effects, smooth scroll animations, and a contact form.

## Sections

### 1. Navigation Bar
- Fixed top navbar with logo, company name, and section links (About, Services, Projects, Contact)
- Mobile hamburger menu
- Smooth scroll to sections

### 2. Hero Section
- Full-width hero with soft blue gradient background
- Logo image centered/left
- Company name "NXTQUIK" with tagline and description
- Two CTA buttons: "Get Started" and "Schedule Meeting" (both scroll to contact form)
- Subtle animated gradient or floating shapes

### 3. About Us Section
- Company story and vision – innovative, growing startup narrative
- Three feature cards: Innovative Approach, Tailored Solutions, Passionate Team
- "Growing day by day" mention
- **Founder spotlight**: Shubham Sonwal photo (uploaded image) with title "Founder & Vision Lead"
- His bio about passion for technology, development, digital solutions
- His contact: phone and Instagram link
- Clean card layout with glassmorphism effect

### 4. Our Services Section
- Two categories: IT Solutions (7 services) and Content Creation (4 services)
- Icon cards with rounded corners, soft shadows
- Grid layout, responsive

### 5. Our Projects Section
- Portfolio cards for Astrousers, Gabru Looks, Scalvea, and "More Coming Soon"
- Gradient placeholder images with project names
- Brief descriptions

### 6. Contact / Schedule Meeting Section
- Form with: Name, Email, Phone, Company Name, Service Required (dropdown), Message, Date & Time picker
- On submit: sends email to nxtquik@gmail.com (will use mailto or email service)
- Success toast message

### 7. Social Links
- LinkedIn and Instagram buttons with icons

### 8. Footer
- Address, phone, email, copyright
- Social links repeated

## Design
- Color palette: Blues (#1a73e8, #0d47a1, #4fc3f7) with soft gradients
- White backgrounds with light blue/purple gradient accents
- Rounded cards, soft shadows, glassmorphism panels
- Modern sans-serif typography
- Scroll animations using CSS/intersection observer
- Fully responsive

## Email Handling
- For the contact form, we'll use a mailto fallback initially. If the user wants actual email delivery, we can set up Lovable Cloud later.
